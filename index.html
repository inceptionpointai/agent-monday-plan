01 — The Concept

Built on Documents, Not Speculation

Agent Monday is an AI true crime correspondent that reports exclusively from public records. Think Dragnet meets modern investigative podcast — just the facts, delivered with gravity and dry wit.

  • Only source: court filings, arrest reports, government documents
  • Every claim is verifiable. Every story starts with a filing.
  • No anonymous tips. No unnamed sources. No speculation.
  • The differentiator: credibility through radical transparency
  • One filing becomes a podcast, a video, a reel, a thread

Additional Data Points Monday Would Pull

  • Prior record check — any previous arrests in FL or CA
  • FL Statute 316.193 — penalties, mandatory minimums
  • Jupiter PD booking log — time of arrest, booking number
  • Toxicology lab — which facility, expected turnaround
  • Court assignment — which judge, first appearance date
  • Vehicle registration — ownership, any liens
  • Civil case cross-reference — pending lawsuits
  • Attorney of record — firm, notable prior representations
